Sponge Cake with Fruit Cream Filling

Ingredients:
1000g egg
400g sugar
400g low gluten flour
100g melted unsalted butter
1000g cream
100g icing sugar

Method:
Beat egg and sugar together until fluffy. Sift flour into mixture, mix in slowly. Add in melted butter while mixing at the right speed, too fast the butter will sink, too slow it will clump. Bake.

Whip cream and icing sugar at 10:1 ratio, e.g. 1000g cream and 100g icing sugar. Mix in canned fruit salad for filling layer between sponge cake layers. Use softer fruit to decorate the cake, such as kiwifruit, mango, strawberry.
